Casual Leather Belts

Often men are confused about how to style casual leather belts with their outfits effortlessly. Here are a few tips we can offer you to make the right choice:

Never style your casual outfits with a dress or formal belts. Stick to Causal Leather Belts. Make sure you buy a full-grain/top-grain leather belt. You can never go wrong with it.

Three major colours for causal belts are Black, Brown, and Tan. Choose a thick belt (40 mm width) to go with your casual outfits. Make sure to look out for the best colour combinations. It is always a good idea to match the colour of shoes and belts. Often you can style contrasting colour belts with shoes.

The thing about casual leather belts is they go with jeans, chinos and even trousers. You can never go wrong with them.
